The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world and a symbol of love. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The monument is made of beautiful white marble and features stunning carvings and gardens. It is located in Agra, India, and is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
